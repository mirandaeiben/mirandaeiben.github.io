Dr. Miranda Eiben, PhD

Intro

Hi! My name is Miranda Eiben and I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Iceland.

I work on polymer optical materials for millimeter experiments. I currently haunt the CMBeam group. I was previously in the Harvard CMB Lab working on the BICEP/Keck CMB experiments and the UMass Amherst Cryogenics Devices lab working on the TolTEC instrument.
